1. How We Collect and Use Your Information (Including Personal Information)

Personal information refers to various types of information recorded electronically or otherwise that can identify a specific natural person, either alone or in combination with other data. Sensitive personal information may include biometric data (such as fingerprints, facial features, voiceprints), ID numbers, bank account details, financial and transaction data, and location information (e.g., home or work address).

We only collect and use your information for the purposes listed in this statement:

a) Making calls and sending messages: To enable these functions, we collect contact names from your address book, anonymized device identifiers, mobile network codes, and country codes. Contact group attributes, names, and phone numbers are processed locally and never uploaded.

b) Weather queries and task appointments: We collect your location information to enable these features, including but not limited to: current location, frequently visited places, home and work/study locations.

c) Calendar access: To query and manage your schedule, we collect and process your calendar data locally, without uploading it.

d) Third-party app integration: To offer richer services, we may connect with third-party apps via cloud APIs. If a third-party service is needed (e.g., navigation or ticket booking), we may share necessary data such as voice recognition results or location information. If redirected to a third-party service, please read their terms and privacy policy carefully, as we are not responsible for their practices.
